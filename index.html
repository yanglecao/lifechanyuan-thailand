Welcome to Lifechanyuan Thailand

Lifechanyuan is a peaceful and spiritually focused community where individuals come together to live harmoniously, pursue spiritual growth, and foster an environment of love and freedom.

About Us

Our community embraces a lifestyle free from traditional family structures, aiming for a life of shared joy and mutual respect. Each member has the opportunity to explore their spiritual path, contribute to shared responsibilities, and grow through daily practices such as meditation, Tai Chi, and cooperative activities.
